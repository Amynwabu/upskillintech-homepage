## Selected Approach: Modern Professional (Response 3)

**Chosen design philosophy**: Modern Professional — Clean Conversion-Focused Landing Page

This approach best serves UpskillinTech's goal of communicating AI transformation to working professionals. The clean layout with teal/green brand colors, warm photography, and clear visual hierarchy will guide visitors from awareness to conversion. The asymmetric split layouts avoid the "AI slop" of centered purple gradients while maintaining the professional credibility the brand needs.
